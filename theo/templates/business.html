Growth Strategy

Our approach to market expansion follows a three-phase strategy:

Phase 1: Foundation

Target partnerships with innovative universities and corporate training departments to refine our product and establish case studies.

  • Establish 5-10 flagship implementations
  • Gather comprehensive user feedback
  • Refine core technology and UX

Phase 2: Expansion

Broaden our customer base while developing specialized modules for different educational contexts.

  • Expand sales and marketing efforts
  • Develop domain-specific knowledge graphs
  • Add enterprise integration capabilities

Phase 3: Ecosystem

Create an open platform where third-party developers can extend Lyceum with specialized modules and integrations.

  • Launch developer API program
  • Establish marketplace for extensions
  • Foster educational technology community
